Teaching Philosophy

Every student who takes a composition course will write in their future – whether it’s a PhD dissertation or emails to coworkers – so the claim that any student is not “a writer” is inherently false. Writing pedagogy, then, should focus on the implicit learnings possible from such courses, rather than how “right” a student can “get” a given assignment...
